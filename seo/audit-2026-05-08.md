## Phase plan

- **Phase 3a (today):** Add og:image set + og:site_name + og:locale, twitter card, geo meta, full @graph schema (SportsOrganization + WebSite + page-type + BreadcrumbList), skip-link + main wrapper + in-body breadcrumb nav across all 13 pages.
- **Phase 3b (today):** Create sitemap.xml and robots.txt at site root.
- **Phase 3c (today):** Commit + push to GitHub. Cloudflare Pages auto-deploys.
- **Phase 2 (backlog):** Voice references not applicable in the same way (sports club, not service business). If pursued, build voice/stats/opinions/stories from club history, founding member quotes, championship narratives.
- **Phase 4 (backlog):** Content expansion. Most pages are already long-form. Could add an FAQ section to get-involved.html (registration timing, age cutoffs, fees, training nights).
- **Phase 5 (n/a):** Not a service business with city LPs - one home ground, one catchment.

## Anti-patterns avoided

- No `aggregateRating` introduced (no verified single-platform review count).
- No fabricated stats. Founding 1975 is verified from club records.
- No em dashes.
- No exclamation marks, hashtags, or emojis added to copy.
