Junior Football

Where the
ANSC story starts

The junior section runs ten teams across the 2026 season, from U8 MiniRoos taking their first kicks through to U14 Juniors mixing it in age-group football. We compete in Coles MiniRoos Mixed Sunday and Junior Mixed Sunday under Football Victoria, with squads named after Spanish footballing locales (Masnou and Oriol) and the Aussie classics (Kangaroos and Wallabies) to distinguish multiple teams in the same age group.

Junior football at ANSC is built on the same values as the senior section: family-friendly, welcoming, and proud of where it comes from. Every senior player you see at Duane Reserve was once a junior somewhere - we are committed to giving the next generation the same start.

MiniRoos
U6 to U11

Football Victoria's MiniRoos program is grassroots football done properly. Small-sided games, equal time on the pitch, no scores or ladders, and a focus on touches, fun, and friendships. ANSC runs five MiniRoos sides across U8, U9, and U10 in 2026.

Junior Home Ground

AB Shaw
Reserve

Junior football at ANSC is based at AB Shaw Reserve in Laverton. Training nights and Sunday morning fixtures all run out of AB Shaw, with full pitches for the older age groups and small-sided setups for the MiniRoos.

It is a separate ground from the senior section's home at Duane Reserve, but it carries the same colours, the same culture, and the same Sunday morning energy that has defined ANSC junior football for decades.
