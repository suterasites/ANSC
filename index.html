About the Club

Welcome to the
Aya Blues

Altona North Soccer Club has been a cornerstone of football in Melbourne's western suburbs since 1975. Based at D.N. Duane Reserve in Brooklyn, within the City of Hobsons Bay, we are a family-oriented club that welcomes players of all ages and skill levels.

Our club prides itself on building community through the game. Whether you are a seasoned player stepping onto the pitch in State League 2 or a young footballer taking your first touch in our Miniroos program, you will find a place at Altona North. We value camaraderie, development, and the shared love of football that brings us together every week.

Our Story

Nearly 50 Years of
Community Football

Founded in 1975, Altona North Soccer Club was built by the local community in Brooklyn - families and footballers who wanted a club to call their own in Melbourne's western suburbs.

From humble beginnings on the fields of Duane Reserve, the club has grown into a proud competitor in Football Victoria's league structure. Our journey through the ranks - from State League 4 champions in 2020 to State League 3 champions in 2025 - reflects the dedication and passion of everyone who has pulled on the navy blue jersey.

Today, the Aya Blues continue that tradition, fielding senior and junior teams that carry the spirit of the club's founders onto the pitch every match day.
