About the Club

Welcome to
Altona North SC

Founded in 1975 and based at D.N. Duane Reserve in Brooklyn, Altona North Soccer Club is a family-oriented club proudly representing Melbourne's western suburbs in Football Victoria State League 2 North/West. We are a welcoming, inclusive club that caters to players of all ages and skill levels.

From our senior men's squad to our junior elite teams and Miniroos programs, we offer a structured pathway for every player to grow, compete, and belong. ANSC is built on community, camaraderie, and a shared love of the game that has brought people together at Duane Reserve for over 50 years.

Our Story

Nearly 50 Years of
Community Football

Founded in 1975, Altona North Soccer Club was built by the local community in Brooklyn - families and footballers who wanted a club to call their own in Melbourne's western suburbs.

From humble beginnings on the fields of Duane Reserve, the club has grown into a proud competitor in Football Victoria's league structure. Our journey through the ranks - from State League 4 champions in 2020 to State League 3 champions in 2025 - reflects the dedication and passion of everyone who has pulled on the navy blue jersey.

Today, ANSC continues that tradition, fielding senior and junior teams that carry the spirit of the club's founders onto the pitch every match day.
